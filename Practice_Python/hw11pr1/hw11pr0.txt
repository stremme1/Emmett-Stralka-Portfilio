Some examples of where computers have made humans better at something that has traditionally been a characteristically human strength include:

-Helping people learn new languages more quickly and effectively
-Enabling people to become better researchers and analysts by providing quick and easy access to large amounts of data
-Allowing people to create and share information more easily and efficiently
-Making it possible for people to communicate with each other more effectively


Some examples of where our aesthetic intuition may be limiting our success unnecessarily include:

-In fashion, where people may be hesitant to try new styles that they think are "ugly"
-In art, where people may be resistant to new styles or forms that they consider to be ugly or strange
-In architecture, where people may be unwilling to consider new designs that they find unattractive
